KALKI AVATAR

In Hinduism, Kalki is the prophesied tenth and final incarnation of the god Vishnu, the preserver of the universe. Kalki is described as a warrior who will appear during the Kali Yuga, a period of darkness and chaos, to end evil and usher in a new age of peace and harmony
